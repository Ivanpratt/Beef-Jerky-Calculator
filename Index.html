The Beef Jerky Calculator

Welcome to the Beef Jerky Calculator. Enter in the desired characteristics of your Beef Jerky (Smoky, Spicy, Sweet, etc), then select the items you wish you create the Beef Jerky with. The Beef Jerky Calculator will then determine the amount of ingredients you need to work with in order to match your desired jerky characteristics. Afterwards, a full-paged instructional recipe will be displayed.
